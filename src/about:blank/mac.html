about:blank macOS installation instruction

Open Safari.

Go to the top menu bar & click on Safari.

Select 'Preferences...' in the dropdown.

A new window should appear now. Click on 'Extensions' in the top bar of that window.

You should see about:blank on the left side now of the window now. Check the checkbox to activate about:blank.

Make sure that you have about:blank installed on your iOS device to manage your rules.
